Idea Convergence/Divergence — Northwestern NICO

With Dr. Evey Huang (Uzzi group). We’re building a pipeline to detect convergence bursts, track idea IDs, compare human ↔ LLM codes, and summarize actionable insights.

What I’m building

  • Temporal detection of convergence/divergence from transcripts.
  • Agreement metrics (Cohen’s κ) for Max ↔ Evey ↔ LLM.
  • Summaries that surface solution‑oriented thinking.

Status

Step 1 complete (burst detection + extraction). Step 2 in progress (summarization + insight tagging).
