ALISSTAIR — UCLA STATS 10

ALISSTAIR (Artificial Language Intelligence System for Statistical Teaching Aid and Individualized Response) is an LLM‑guided set of labs and helpers designed to support introductory statistics learners. We focused on clarity, step‑by‑step feedback, and lightweight evaluation to inform improvements across the quarter.

My role

  • Co‑designed lab prompts and grading rubrics to align with course objectives.
  • Built evaluation checks to track misunderstanding patterns and iterate on prompts.
  • Coordinated rollout & documentation for TAs and students.
