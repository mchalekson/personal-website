Undergraduate TA — UCLA STATS 20

As a Learning Intern (Undergraduate Teaching Assistant) for UCLA’s STATS 20, I supported over 320 students per quarter in mastering R programming, data manipulation, and foundational data‑science concepts.

Winter 2023 – Spring 2024, Summer 2024, Fall 2024 • Instructors: Dr. Michael Tsiang, Dr. Thomas Maierhofer, Dr. Ian McGovern, Dr. Edouardo Honig.

Key contributions

  • Led 12+ hrs/week of group learning, office hours, and exam reviews.
  • Assessed midterms (free‑response) and delivered actionable feedback.
  • Designed & refined materials (practice problems, midterms, final reviews) aligned to outcomes.
  • Partnered with faculty & PhD students to analyze student challenges and adjust instruction.
