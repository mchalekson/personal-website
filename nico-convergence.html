Multimodal Scientific Team Collaboration Research — NICO

This ongoing project is conducted under Dr. Evey Huang at the Northwestern Institute on Complex Systems (NICO), in collaboration with Prof. Brian Uzzi. Our research investigates how multimodal AI can help teams reason, align, and generate knowledge in scientific contexts.
