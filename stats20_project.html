Undergraduate TA – UCLA STATS 20

As a Learning Intern (Undergraduate Teaching Assistant) for UCLA's STATS 20, I supported over 320 students per quarter in mastering statistical programming in R, data manipulation, and fundamental data science concepts.

Served as a TA in Winter 2023 – Spring 2024, Summer 2024, Fall 2024, under the instruction of Dr. Michael Tsiang, Dr. Thomas Maierhofer, Dr. Ian McGovern, Dr. Edouardo Honig.

Key Contributions

Led 12+ hours per week of group learning, office hours, and exam review sessions.
Assisted in grading midterm exams , evaluating free-response questions, and providing detailed feedback.
Designed and refined practice problems, midterms, and final review materials aligned with course objectives.
Worked with faculty and PhD students to analyze student challenges and adjust instructional methods for better learning outcomes.
