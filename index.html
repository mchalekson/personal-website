About

I’m a Data Science M.S. student at Northwestern University focused on human–AI collaboration and social computing. My work looks at how teams build shared understanding over time using speech, gesture, and what’s on the screen.

I focus on making team behavior interpretable and measurable. Lately that’s meant LLM-assisted annotation, convergence detection, and tooling for multimodal meeting analysis.

Outside of research, I’m usually bouldering, training for distance running, taking portraits, or cooking.

You can contact me at mchalekson@u.northwestern.edu.
